Facilities for Students

Facilities For Students coming to undertake Yoga course at our centre:

  • International and Domestic Air Tickets for both side journey.
  • We provide Rail Ticket, Bus Ticket or requirement of Taxi also.
  • Pick and Drop facility from airport, railway station or bus stand to yoga center(for Delhi airport, Haridwar, Dheradun, Rishikesh) and vice-versa.
  • Pure and Hygienic vegetarian food, drinking R.O water. In addition, we provide self-cooking facilities also. 
  • If any student wishes to visit any part of India, we provide packages for that too. A guide will accompany the students in their sightseeing visits. 
  • Vissa Facilities for Yoga students. Here, Terms and conditions apply.
  • Full Security for all students. And some special arrangements for Female students.
  • If a student suffers from any medical problem, we provide doctor and medical facilities for students.
  • Student undertaking any type of course at our centre will be provided with the lifetime membership by our center free of cost and can undergo regular yoga classes free of cost once undergoing any TTC course in any of the branch of our centre.
  • If any student wishes to join with us and want to do job at our center in any department, we also provides facilities to such students and will be given salary based on their work.
  • If any student after undertaking course at our centre, wishes to open his yoga studio in his country we provide facility for that person to work with us in partnership in which we will provide the respected teachers, syllabus, certificates etc..

PARTNERSHIP AGREEMENT:

1. In the partnership, Om Yoga will offer its franchise all the courses that Om Yoga takes in its own center along with their details, curriculum, logos, some other standards of Om Yoga and its partner World Yoga Alliance. 
And the most important thing Om Yoga will provide the registered license to the registered students in its franchise center 10 days within from the day student has been registered at Om Yoga by its franchise center.

2. Anytime, Franchise center requires the teachers(for any type of course, Lead trainer or Asans Teacher) from Om Yoga, will be provided to you but required to be informed before 45 days the starting of course. And for that, Franchise center has to offer the teacher, accommodation for full duration course, 3 times meal, Air ticket(economy class) and visa. And 25% of the course fee per student(for lead trainer) and 15% of the course fee per student(for only Asanas and Pranayama trainer, Asanas Theory). For this, minimum students in group should be 15.

3. Franchise Center needs to have a proper space for Yoga hall  and accommodation facility, food facility. A website should be there wherein all the details should be mentioned about Yoga center and its courses, And a registered mail id. And also, organization to get registered in local country.And a provision of medical facilities for students must be there.

4. This agreement is only with the Om Yoga International for imparting Yoga education to students and not with the organization Adhyatm Anusandhan Yog Trust for any other purpose.
5. Agreement is valid for a minimum of 3 years after which it will be renewed.
6. No interference by the other party will be made in the courses details and syllabus as mentioned by Om Yoga center. Suggestions will be most welcomed.
7. Use of logos and other copyright marks such as Details of Syllabus and its content is not liable to provide it to any third party.
8. In future, if party tries to apart from Om Yoga Center, will not be liable to use any of the logos, details or any of the syllabus and Email-id given by Om Yoga. 
9. The time the student is registered with Om Yoga and registration form is sent to us, along with registration form , our share of money should be transferred along with the form keeping your share with yourself.
10. Should provide one passport size photograph at the time of  sending registration form to Om Yoga.
11.  Syllabus for the course will be issued to the student only after receiving the total fees of the course and before only details of the course can be provided. 
12. Both the parties agree to maintain the confidentiality and at no time disclose any information to the third party without the prior consent of other party.
13. Either Part may terminate this Agreement if it discovers at any stage that the other party is in violation of any laws or regulations.
14. If other party has any suggestions or want to have change in any of the system, we can have a meeting over that and after discussion can finalize the point.

 

Note: Om Yoga International Center is one big family. Any member or student coming to our center will be treated as like a family member.
